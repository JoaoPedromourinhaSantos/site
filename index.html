Lesandro Ponciano

I have a PhD degree in Computer Science. My research and teaching activities are in Human-Computer Interaction, Collective intelligence, and Distributed Computing areas. I study human engagement and credibility in sociotechnical systems using quantitative methods, including behavioural modelling, data-driven simulation and machine learning. Some of my efforts are meta-research on crowdsourced citizen science and open science.
