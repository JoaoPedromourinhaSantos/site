Lesandro Ponciano

I have a PhD degree in Computer Science. My research is in Human-Computer Interaction, Information Systems, and Distributed Computing. My previous experiences are mainly with quantitative analysis, including system simulation, data mining, and data modeling algorithms. I have been working on characterizing and assessing human engagement and credibility in Sociotechnical Systems and Citizen Science Projects.
